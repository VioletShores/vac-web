🕐 Best fishing windows today

Two hours either side of high tide works well — 6:30 am – 10:30 am and again 6:45 pm – 10:45 pm. The incoming tide in the morning is usually the most active.

What's biting where

🐟
Bream
The pylons and rock ledges under the Spit Bridge. Use prawns or hardbody lures on the outgoing tide.
🐠
Flathead
Sandy bottom around Clontarf Beach and the channel. Soft plastics on the bottom work well in the morning.
🐡
Whiting
The shallow flats toward Sandy Bay. Beach worms or pippis, keep the bait small and on the sand.
