How long sessions remain valid before requiring re-verification.

QUICK_REAUTH_TTL_MINUTES
30 min
Quick re-auth (finger count) session lifetime. Shorter window for low-friction re-verification. env: VAC_QUICK_REAUTH_TTL_MINUTES
OTP_EXPIRY_MINUTES
10 min
One-time passcode validity window. OTPs expire after this duration regardless of use.

Staleness Thresholds
Verification freshness tiers — trust decays as sessions age. (Patent Claims 16–38)
FRESHNESS_TIER_FULL_HOURS
1 hr
Verification within this window is considered FULL trust. Highest freshness tier. env: VAC_FRESHNESS_FULL_HOURS
FRESHNESS_TIER_HIGH_HOURS
24 hr
Verification within this window is considered HIGH trust. Same-day freshness. env: VAC_FRESHNESS_HIGH_HOURS
FRESHNESS_TIER_MODERATE_HOURS
168 hr (7d)
Verification within this window is considered MODERATE trust. Week-old freshness. env: VAC_FRESHNESS_MODERATE_HOURS
FRESHNESS_TIER_AGING_HOURS
720 hr (30d)
Verification within this window is considered AGING trust. Month-old — near expiry. env: VAC_FRESHNESS_AGING_HOURS

Biometric Thresholds
Confidence and matching thresholds for liveness, deepfake, and lip-sync scoring.
LIVENESS_BLINK_THRESHOLD
0.80
Gemini confidence score required to pass liveness check. Below this = challenge fails.
DEEPFAKE_CONFIDENCE_THRESHOLD
0.50
Below this confidence = flagged as suspicious. Used during deepfake detection stage.
CHALLENGE_WORD_MATCH_THRESHOLD
0.60
Fraction of core words that must match the spoken challenge. Lowered for accent tolerance — 5 other modalities carry identity proof.
LIP_SYNC_MISMATCH_PENALTY
0.50×
Multiply biometric confidence by this factor when lip sync doesn't match audio.
LIP_SYNC_MISMATCH_CAP
0.50
Maximum score achievable when lip-sync doesn't match. Hard cap regardless of other signals.
