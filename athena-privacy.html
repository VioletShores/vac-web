Privacy & your data

Plain language. No legal tricks. Here's exactly what happens with your data when you use Athena Co-Pilot, powered by VAC Protocol.
The most important thing

We store a text description of your appearance — not your photo or video.

When you verify your identity, our AI analyses your face and voice in real-time and creates a short text description (e.g. "person with brown hair and glasses"). The video recording is processed and immediately discarded. No facial images, no voice recordings, no video files are stored on any server.

What we collect

Identity

Email address — used for sign-in codes and vouch requests.

Text face description — generated by AI from your verification video. Used for re-authentication ("is this the same person?"). No raw images stored.

Name — as you provide it. Used for greetings and vouch requests.

Trust chain

Vouch records — who vouched for whom, when. This is the cryptographic trust chain that makes the system work. Vouches are permanent and auditable.

Trust level — unverified, verified, or trusted. Based on number of vouches received.

App usage

Feedback you submit — text and voice feedback you explicitly provide to improve your copilot.

App interactions — which sections you use, what you find helpful. Used to personalise your experience.

Onboarding responses — answers you provide during personalisation (interests, preferences, tools you use).

What we never collect

Never stored
  • Facial images or photographs
  • Voice recordings or audio files
  • Video files from verification
  • Passwords (we don't have passwords — just biometric + email codes)
  • Location data (unless you explicitly share it)
  • Data from other apps on your phone

Where your data lives

Infrastructure

Backend: Railway (cloud hosting, Sydney region preference when available). Data stored in encrypted SQLite database.

Frontend: Vercel (global CDN). No user data stored on frontend servers.

AI processing: Google Gemini (face analysis) and Deepgram (voice transcription). Data is processed in real-time and not retained by these providers for training or other purposes.

Your rights

You control your data

Access: Ask us what data we hold about you at any time.

Deletion: Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data. We'll confirm deletion within 48 hours.

Portability: Request a copy of your data in a standard format.

Correction: Update or correct any information we hold.

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@violetshores.com

For medical app users

Additional protections

If you use a health-related copilot (e.g. Zagarella Dermatology), additional protections apply:

  • Clinical images you upload are processed in-session and not stored permanently unless you explicitly request it
  • Health information is handled in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and Australian Privacy Principles (APPs)
  • We comply with the My Health Records Act 2012 where applicable
  • Your health data is never shared with insurance companies, employers, or any third party without your explicit written consent

Country-specific

Applicable frameworks

Australia & New Zealand: Privacy Act 1988 (AU), Privacy Act 2020 (NZ), Australian Privacy Principles.

European Union: General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You have additional rights including right to object to processing and right to restrict processing.

United States: We comply with applicable state privacy laws including CCPA (California), VCDPA (Virginia), and CPA (Colorado).

Is anything unclear?

If anything about our privacy practices is confusing, concerning, or missing — tell us. We take this seriously.
